About Us

Independent hearing care with a personal touch

We’re a family-run hearing care practice serving Hertfordshire. No corporate targets, no hard sell — just honest, dedicated audiology from people who genuinely care.

Our Story

A family practice, not a chain

Halo Hearing Solutions is an independent, family-run hearing care practice based in the heart of Hertfordshire. We opened our doors because we believe everyone deserves access to honest, high-quality hearing care — without the corporate pressure that comes with the big chains.

Our HCPC registered audiologists take the time to truly listen to every patient. We don’t rush appointments, we don’t push products you don’t need, and we never use hard-sell tactics. Instead, we focus on building lasting relationships with the communities we serve across Berkhamsted, Bovingdon, and Chorleywood.

From ear wax removal to comprehensive hearing assessments and expertly fitted hearing aids, we’re dedicated to providing personal, professional care that makes a real difference to your everyday life.
