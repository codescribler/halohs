02

What We Found

We spent time reviewing your current website, your Google Business Profiles, and how you compare to other hearing care providers in Hertfordshire. Here’s what stood out:

Your reputation is exceptional — 5.0 stars, patients praising the personal care, the warmth, the honesty. That’s rare, and it’s genuinely your strongest competitive advantage. But your website isn’t telling that story. When a potential patient lands on your site, this is what they experience:

  • Hard-to-read text in key areas — when someone first arrives, some of the text blends into the background. If a visitor can’t instantly read what you do and how to book, they leave
  • The homepage slideshow is losing you bookings — the rotating banner at the top looks polished, but study after study shows that slideshows actively reduce the number of people who take action. Most visitors never see past the first slide, and the movement draws attention away from your booking buttons and phone numbers
  • Dated visual design — doesn’t reflect the premium quality of your in-clinic experience
  • Slow to load — heavy plugins and large images mean visitors are left waiting, and some won’t
  • Doesn’t work well on phones — many elements don’t adapt to smaller screens, yet this is how most patients will find you
  • Booking buttons aren’t prominent enough — visitors have to scroll and search to find how to contact you or book an appointment
  • Google can’t showcase you properly — the site is missing the behind-the-scenes information that helps Google display your star rating, location, and services in search results
  • Your 5-star reviews are hidden — your reviews are your strongest asset, yet a new visitor won’t see them unless they go looking

The gap between your online presence and your real-world reputation is the opportunity here. People searching “ear wax removal near me” or “hearing test Berkhamsted” need to land on a site that immediately builds trust and makes booking effortless. Right now, some of those people are choosing a competitor with a better website — not a better service.

What We're Proposing

A complete, ground-up website redesign built around three priorities:

1. Trust at First Glance

Every page leads with your strongest assets: your 5-star rating, HCPC registration, and the warmth of your patient reviews. Visitors should feel the quality of your practice within 3 seconds of landing.

2. Effortless Booking

“Book Now” and phone buttons visible on every page, on every device. On mobile, booking and call buttons stay pinned to the bottom of the screen so they’re always one tap away. We remove every possible barrier between “I need help” and “I’ve booked.”

3. Premium Feel, Personal Warmth

Your signature teal is the foundation, paired with warm cream tones and a subtle gold accent for a premium feel. The typography is chosen for elegance and readability — especially for your audience. Plenty of breathing room, smooth transitions, and photography that feels genuinely human.

Six Pages, Fully Designed

  • Home — strong first impression with your headline, trust signals, services, patient reviews, and locations
  • Ear Wax Removal — clear pricing, what to expect at the appointment, and frequently asked questions
  • Hearing Loss & Hearing Aids — reassuring, educational content that helps patients understand their options
  • About Us — your story, values, team, and HCPC credentials
  • Reviews — your best patient testimonials, organised by service and location
  • Contact — enquiry form, all three locations with maps, and opening hours

What This Means for Your Practice

Patients Won’t Wait — and They Won’t Have To

Your current site takes several seconds to load. The new site loads in under 2 seconds. That matters because research shows that over half of mobile visitors leave a site that takes more than 3 seconds to appear. Faster loading means fewer people giving up before they even see your services.

More Patients Finding You on Google

Each service gets its own page, targeting the exact searches your patients are making — “ear wax removal Berkhamsted”, “hearing test near me”, “audiologist Chorleywood”. Behind the scenes, we include the data Google needs to display your star rating, address, and opening hours directly in search results — making your listing stand out before anyone even clicks through.

More Visitors Actually Booking

Every design decision is made with one question in mind: does this make it easier for someone to book? Phone numbers are tappable on mobile. Booking buttons appear throughout the site and stay pinned to the screen on phones. Your best reviews appear at the moments when a visitor is deciding whether to take the next step. The result: a higher proportion of visitors become booked patients.

A Site That Works for All Your Patients

Many of your patients are older or may have additional needs. The site is designed to be easy to read, easy to navigate, and comfortable for everyone — including visitors using screen readers, keyboards, or those who find screen animations uncomfortable. No one is excluded.
